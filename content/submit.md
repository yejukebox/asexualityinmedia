## Contribute to Our Database

Know of an asexual character in television or film that's not in our database? We'd love to hear about them! Your contributions help make this resource more comprehensive and valuable for the community.

### How to Submit

We use GitHub Issues to manage character submissions. This allows for transparent review and discussion before adding characters to the database.

**[Click here to submit a character →](https://github.com/yejukebox/asexualityinmedia/issues/new?assignees=&labels=character-submission&template=character-submission.yml&title=%5BCharacter%5D%3A+)**

### What We Need

When submitting a character, please provide:

- **Character Full Name**: The character's complete name
- **Nickname**: Any commonly used nickname or alias
- **Show/Film Title**: The name of the television show or film
- **Genre**: The primary genre (Animation, Drama, Comedy, Fantasy, etc.)
- **Release Year**: The year the show first aired or the film was released
- **Rating**: Content rating (TV-MA, TV-14, TV-PG, PG-13, R, etc.)
- **Language**: Primary language of the media
- **Representation Type**:
  - Explicit: Character explicitly identifies as asexual
  - Implicit: Character's portrayal suggests asexual representation
- **Evidence/Justification**: Brief explanation of why this character should be included (specific episodes, creator statements, character analysis, etc.)

### Submission Guidelines

#### Explicit Representation
Characters should have at least one of the following:
- Direct statement of asexual identity in dialogue
- Creator/writer confirmation of asexual identity
- Clear narrative elements establishing asexuality

#### Implicit Representation
For implicit representation, please provide:
- Consistent pattern of behavior across multiple episodes/scenes
- Context about the character's relationships and development
- Why the portrayal aligns with asexual experiences

### Review Process

1. You submit the character through our GitHub Issue form
2. Community members and maintainers review the submission
3. Discussion occurs if there are questions or clarifications needed
4. If approved, the character is added to the database
5. The website is updated with the new character

### Quality Standards

We aim to maintain a high-quality database. Submissions should:

- Include verifiable information
- Provide sufficient evidence for the classification
- Be from released media (not upcoming shows/films)
- Represent genuine asexual representation or coding

### Questions?

If you have questions about the submission process or criteria, please:
- Check our [About](/about/) page for more information
- Open a general issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/yejukebox/asexualityinmedia/issues)
- Join the discussion on existing submissions

### Thank You!

Your contributions help build a comprehensive resource for the asexual community and allies. Every submission, whether accepted or discussed, helps us refine our understanding of ace representation in media.
